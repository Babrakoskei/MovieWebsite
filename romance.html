365 Days

Massimo is a member of the Sicilian Mafia family and Laura is a sales director. She does not expect that on a trip to Sicily trying to save her relationship, Massimo will kidnap her and give her 365 days to fall in love with him.

Fifty Shades

Anastasia Steele steps in for her sick roommate to interview prominent businessman Christian Grey for their campus paper, little does she realize the path her life will take. Christian, as enigmatic as he is rich and powerful, finds himself strangely drawn to Ana, and she to him. Though sexually inexperienced, Ana plunges headlong into an affair and learns that Christian's true sexual proclivities push the boundaries of pain and pleasure.

Me Before You

After becoming unemployed, vivacious and unassuming Louisa Clark (Lou) faces pointed questions of the heart when she takes a job caring for a wealthy, cynical bachelor, Will Traynor,who was paralyzed in an accident. The two of them soonbond with each other. The movie has some pretty heavy themes going on. Although it's not based on any one true story, it was inspired by the real suffering and dilemmas of people with disabilities.

Sex Next Door

The personal and professional lives of four sex workers in Seattle, including an exploration of the stigma and pressures they face.This short-form documentary series humanizes sex workers while de-stigmatizing a compelling range of alternative sexualities.

Tallgirl

Jodi Kreyman is 16 and 6 ft 1+1⁄2 in (1.87 m) tall. She has been tall for her age since she was three, which has made her insecure her whole life. In contrast, Jodi's older sister Harper is of average height and a multiple beauty pageant winner. Dunkleman, a life-long friend, frequently asks her out, but she is reluctant, partly because he's much shorter than her. Stig Mohlin, a Swedish foreign exchange student, joins Jodi's class, and she is immediately interested.

The Fault in our Stars

If you like feel-good, escapist films, this one's not for you. The Fault in Our Stars is one of the most unpretentious tragic love stories told in a heart-warming manner. Two cancer-afflicted teenagers Hazel and Augustus meet at a cancer support group. The two of them embark on a journey to visit a reclusive author in Amsterdam.

The Space Between Us

The Space Between Us is a romantic drama that tempts fate and defies gravity in the best Sci-Fi romance sort of way. Sarah Elliot, an astronaut, dies on Mars while giving birth to her son. The boy visits Earth and starts searching for his father with the help of a clever girl.
